Instructions

Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock is a zero-sum game, typically played by two opponents.

Players select weapons, each with a certain degree of power, ultimately leading to an outcome.

A typical game consists of a number of rounds where the opponent with the best of 3 winning rounds is victorious.

The game is popular worldwide, played by children and adults alike, and often used to make decisions where no room for cheating or predicting the other person's move is allowed.


Apart from being a fun pastime, Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock is also played in situations where a decision needs to be made, similar to flipping a coin, rolling dice, or drawing straws.

The game's simplicity and unpredictability make it a satisfying way to resolve disputes without the possibility of error or conflict.
